Parent's Guide to Screen Time

Age-Appropriate Guidelines

0-2 years

Avoid screen time except for video chatting with family.

2-5 years

Limit to 1 hour per day of high-quality programming with co-viewing.

6+ years

Consistent limits on time and types of media. Ensure screen time doesn't replace sleep and physical activity.

Healthy Digital Habits

  • Create tech-free zones (bedrooms, dining table)
  • Establish device-free times (meals, before bed)
  • Model healthy screen use behaviors
  • Encourage creative over passive consumption
  • Prioritize face-to-face interactions

Warning Signs

Behavioral Issues

Irritability when not using devices, difficulty transitioning away from screens.

Physical Symptoms

Eye strain, headaches, poor posture, sleep disturbances.

Social Impact

Withdrawal from family activities, declining interest in offline play.
